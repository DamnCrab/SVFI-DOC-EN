What is VFI

# VFI = Video Frame Interpolation

WARNING

This entry needs to be supplemented

The video frame rate is increased by some specific algorithms, that is, new frames that are not included in the original video are inserted between the original frames to make video smoother in motion.

Traditional VFI algorithms are mostly "based" on hardware MEMC, or software based optical flow. SVFI uses advanced artificial intelligence algorithm RIFE and its derivatives (opens new window) for VFI, whose overall performances outperform other algorithms.
